Random Fact

Enchiladas are a traditional Mexican dish. It is made with a fried corn tortilla dipped in red sauce, rolled and filled with different ingredients such as chicken, cheese, carrots and potatoes or beans.

My all-time favorite enchilada filling is fresh cheese with sliced onion. I'm on a mission to find the best enchilada here in Ottawa.
